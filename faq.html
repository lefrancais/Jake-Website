Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Do you cater for Rabbits?
  • A: Not currently but we plan to in the future!

  • Q: What happens if my pet gets sick?
  • A: We are well trained at caring for sick animals and will call you immediately to let you know the situation. We will suggest visiting a vet if we believe necessary but will wait for expressed permission from you, the owner, first.

  • Q: Can we leave more than one pet with you at once?
  • A: Yes of course! We would love to look after all of your pets however each pet must be payed for separately.
